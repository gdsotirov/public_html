Web Applications

Interest Calculator

The application allows calculation of the interest on deposit accounts. It allow calculation for unlimited period of time and shows the accumulation of the interest.

  • URL: http://gsotirov79.ddns.homelan.bg/apps/calc/icalc/
  • WebCVS: projects/web/icalc

Mortgage Calculator

This calculator is like the calculators of most of the banks, but it is entirely Javascript based (no data is transfered from the client to the server) and allows reverse calculation (from the payment it calculates the amount that can be given).

  • URL: http://gsotirov79.ddns.homelan.bg/apps/calc/mcalc/
  • WebCVS: projects/web/icalc
