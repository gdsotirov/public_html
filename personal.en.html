Flights

For some time now I'm recording all my flights in my.flightradar24, which is a great service in this regards. You could keep a flight diary by recording date and time, flight number, direction, plane type (and later its registration) and other information. There is a convenient review and edit of the flight diary and the profile page displays a nice map of routes and various statistical information about your flights. You could grade and review airports, airlines and planes if you will.
