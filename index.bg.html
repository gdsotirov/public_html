Here you can find some info about me, to look at my picture galleries and you can find ways to contact me by email, ICQ or phone. This page is developed to be standards compliant, usable and accessible for all people browsing the web. Please, let me know if you found something that doesn't work in you browser so I can try fix it. You can send me your questions, opinions or recommendations about this page on any of my email addresses.
